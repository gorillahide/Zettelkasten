# Prime Number Theorem
P(n) = # of prime numbers in {2, ..., n}. How big is P(n)?

## Statement:
$\lim_{n \to \inf} \frac{P(n)}{ln(n)} = 1$

### Followup:
How big is | $\frac{P(n)}{ln(n)} - 1$ | for large n? There's a conjecture fore this which is the [[Riemann Hypothesis]]
